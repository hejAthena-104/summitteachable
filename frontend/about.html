Our story

We believe you should learn before you risk

Summit Teachable was built on a simple, demo-first idea: the safest way to learn to trade is to practice in an environment where mistakes cost nothing but lessons.

Our mission

Make trading education accessible — and safe

Too many people are pushed into real-money trading before they understand the basics. We flip that order. Our learners start with structured courses, then practise everything in a risk-free demo dashboard funded with simulated money.

There's no pressure, no deposit required to trade, and no chance of losing real capital while you're still learning. Just a clear path from curious beginner to disciplined, confident trader.
